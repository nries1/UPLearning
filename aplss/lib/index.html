First, make a copy of all PL system docs, scripts and folders. When you click "make a copy" a new folder will be created in your Google Drive, which will contain clean copies of all the docs, sheets, forms, folders, and apps that you'll need to start a new PL system. Once copied, the unique IDs for all of the docs, sheets and folders will be saved for you so you don't have to add them to your code.
